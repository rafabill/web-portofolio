Chelesy!

Chelesy was the first city I ever created!

The campaign starts there. In the top left building the adventurers meet and form parties ,to take on quests together.

Here is what it looks like!

It contains a graveyard, Guild/tavern, blacksmith, houses and a castle of the Royal family.

It was quite the challenge because, I didn't know many of the tools I had available. I created it with an art program called Krita.
